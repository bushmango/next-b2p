Critical Findings
- Critical advisories are all minimist prototype pollution paths.
- yarn why minimist:
  - minimist@1.2.0 is hoisted from:
    - barrel-rider-sidecar > ts-node-dev > minimist
    - barrel-rider-sidecar > ts-node-dev > dateformat > meow > minimist
    - eslint-config-next > eslint-plugin-import > tsconfig-paths > json5 > minimist
  - ts-node-dev > mkdirp > minimist@0.0.8 exists because barrel-rider-sidecar > ts-node-dev > mkdirp depends on it.
  - tsconfig-paths > minimist@1.2.8 exists because eslint-config-next > eslint-plugin-import > tsconfig-paths depends on it.
- The vulnerable critical paths appear to be driven by barrel-rider-sidecar's ts-node-dev dependency tree.
- barrel-rider-sidecar is currently listed as a production dependency even though it is used by the sidecar script/dev workflow.
